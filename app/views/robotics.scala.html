The reason I even started programming, starts here. It was the end of the 5th grade and I planned on joining my middle school robotics team (FLL). So, not knowing what to do we bought a C++ for dummies book. I remember my first real program was a 400 line slot machine game (It was 400 lines because I made an IF statement for literally every case). While my C knowledge was not needed for years to come, I still learned a lot. In FLL I ended up mastering the programming interfaces for the Lego NXT & RCX & becoming the lead programmer. *Reminder to self to upload lego competition pictures*


Following FLL in middle school I joined my high schools robotics FRC team. Year one we used C++ and year two when I took over as lead programmer I switched us to Java. In year 2 I also moved us onto a version control system (git), issue tracker (Github issues) and cleaned up our workflow. I also incorporated new technologies such as SmartDashboard our team never used before which greatly help in debugging. You can find the year 2 (2013) code on github here.
