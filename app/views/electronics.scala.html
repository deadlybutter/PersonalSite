I've looked into purchasing other boards such as the Beaglebone & the new Intel Galileo board but I don't have the funds to do so, but I still keep up with there developments.

I've always had a passion for programming in the physical world which is why I find it fundamental to understand how things function. I'm currently learning more & teaching myself about these topics, so I don't have much to show yet but check back soon!
